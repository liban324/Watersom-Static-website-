Milestone Ceremony

Watersom Solutions & Banaadir Regional Administration Laboratory for Water Safety and Disease Prevention.

A New Chapter for Public Health

The inauguration of the Banaadir Region Water Laboratory marks a landmark moment for water safety in Somalia. Senior officials, scientists, and community leaders gathered to witness the launch of this state-of-the-art facility dedicated to protecting public health.

The laboratory is a result of a Public–Private Partnership between Watersom Solutions and the Banaadir Regional Administration — a commitment to ensuring every drop of water consumed in the region is safe, tested, and trusted.

How We Work

The Complete Water Safety Chain

From field sampling to community protection — an integrated, end-to-end solution.

Water Sampling, Testing & Treatment Cycle Framework

Bacteriological & Chemical Testing

Our laboratory performs comprehensive analysis on every water sample — detecting contamination before it reaches communities.

  • E. coli & faecal coliform detection
  • Chemical: salinity, nitrates, fluoride, heavy metals
  • Physical: turbidity, color, odor, temperature
  • All results benchmarked to WHO standards

Advanced Instrument Analysis

We use cutting-edge equipment including the HACH DR6000 spectrophotometer for precise chemical and physical water quality measurements.

  • Spectrophotometric analysis
  • Chlorine residual measurement
  • Heavy metal detection
  • Real-time digital reporting
Treatment Services

Water Treatment for the Public

Beyond testing, WaterSom Solutions provides direct water treatment interventions for communities, institutions, and public facilities across Somalia. Once a risk is identified, our team mobilises rapidly to neutralise contamination and restore safe, clean water to the people who need it most.

💧

Chlorination & Disinfection

Point-of-use and community-scale chlorination to eliminate bacterial and viral pathogens from drinking water sources.

🌿

Coagulation & Filtration

Removing suspended solids, turbidity, and chemical contaminants through flocculation and multi-stage filtration processes.

🏘️

Community & IDP Camps

Emergency and sustained treatment solutions deployed to internally displaced communities, schools, and health facilities.

🚰

Water Supply Rehabilitation

Supporting municipal authorities in rehabilitating contaminated water supply networks to restore safe distribution to the public.
